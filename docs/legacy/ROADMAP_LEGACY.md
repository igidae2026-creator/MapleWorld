# Roadmap

> Legacy planning document.
> Do not treat this file as top authority.
> Current authority lives in `GOAL.md`, `METAOS_CONSTITUTION.md`, the checklist skeleton, and `docs/standards/DOCUMENTATION_MAP.md`.

Current roadmap is ordered by player-experience bottlenecks, not by maximum feature count.

## Near-Term

- wire explicit machine-readable proxies for `first_10_minutes`, `first_hour_retention`, and `day1_return_intent`
- convert broad gameplay reports into bottleneck-ranked repair targets
- strengthen equipment replacement pressure and economy coherence in the early/mid loop
- deepen party value and early boss goal visibility without broadening world scope

## Mid-Term

- richer boss mechanics and scripted encounter graphs per boss map
- auction settlement queues and offline mail delivery
- content authoring tools that emit registry patches instead of direct source edits
- live balance simulation against telemetry and replay data

## Later

- persistence compaction and multi-revision shard promotion across true multi-channel deployments
- broader world expansion only after quality gates remain stable under repeated runs
