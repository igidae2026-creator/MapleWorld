# World Model

MapleWorld is a single-server, single-world MMORPG runtime.

World structure:

- maps with layered movement routes and region identity
- mobs, rares, elites, and bosses loaded from the content registry
- quests and NPC dialogues that guide regional progression
- social density through shared maps, parties, guilds, raids, and economy surfaces

The server core remains authoritative; the gameplay world extends it through content, combat, progression, social play, economy, and events.
